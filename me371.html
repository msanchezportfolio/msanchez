Remote-Controlled Car

This project involved the development of a small-scale RC car, which featured a custom-built transmission, designed to endure various tests inclduing: speed, agility, strength, and mechanical durability.

Transmission Design: Engineered a constant-mesh system with a custom dog clutch and custom gear trains to enable shifting. Achieved 1 m/s target velocity and capacity for a 2.5 kg inclined haul.

Material Selection: Fabricated a laser-cut acrylic chassis for high-precision component mounting. Additionally, 3D-printed custom wheels using TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) for shock absorption, successfully validating the design against a 5kg impact load.

Optimization: Leveraged 3D printing for majority of components to minimize weight and maintain a <$20 total material cost.
